Social distancing guidelines

1.Restaurants to ensure handwashing protocol and proper cleanliness of frequently touched surfaces. Ensure physical distancing (minimum 1metre) between tables; encourage open air seating where practical with adequate distancing.

2.All commercial activities must keep a distance of one meter between customers. Measures to reduce peak hour crowding in markets/stores.

3.Hygiene and physical distancing has to be maintained. Shaking hands and hugging as a matter of greeting to be avoided.

4. Keep communities informed consistently and constantly.

Credits: Ministry of Health & Family Welfare
