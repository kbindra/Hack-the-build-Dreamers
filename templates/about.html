ABOUT US : CLOSE-O-METER

Our mission is to provide a solution to cope up with the new normal in the wake of the COVID-19 Pandemic!

The website aims to help users to find safe places to visit with live updates about the crowd, statistics of people inside their favourite restaurants, stores and much more!

Live feedbacks and reviews provided by customers help users to select the best suited place for them!

Our vision was to create a website that will help the users to fit into the new normal and help them skip the queues and avoid waiting to visit their favourite places even post-covid!
